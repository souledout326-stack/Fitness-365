🔥 Fitness 365

Daily workouts for body and soul

🏁 Phase 1: Foundation & Fat Burn (Week 1)

Goal: Build cardiovascular endurance, improve mobility, and shed initial weight.

Monday – Full Body Burn
  • 🔁 5 rounds:
  • 30s Jump Rope
  • 10 Squats
  • 10 Push-ups
  • 10 Sit-ups
Tuesday – Core & Cardio
  • 🔁 4 rounds:
  • 1 min Jump Rope
  • 15 Crunches
  • 20 Russian Twists
  • 30s Plank
Wednesday – Lower Body
  • 🔁 4 rounds:
  • 20 Squats
  • 15 Lunges (each leg)
  • 30s Wall Sit
Thursday – Active Recovery
  • 20 min brisk walk or light jump rope
  • Stretching routine
Friday – Upper Body
  • 🔁 4 rounds:
  • 10 Push-ups
  • 10 Shoulder Taps
  • 10 Triceps Dips (use chair)
Saturday – HIIT
  • 🔁 6 rounds:
  • 40s Jump Rope
  • 20s Rest
  • 10 Burpees
  • 10 Mountain Climbers
Sunday – Rest
  • Stretch
  • Hydrate
  • Reflect

🧗 Phase 2: Strength & Skill (Week 2)

Goal: Increase strength, introduce calisthenics basics, and continue fat loss.

Monday – Push Strength
  • 🔁 4 rounds:
  • 10 Incline Push-ups
  • 10 Dips (use chair or bench)
  • 30s Jump Rope
Tuesday – Core Stability
  • 🔁 3 rounds:
  • 20 Leg Raises
  • 30s Plank
  • 20 Flutter Kicks
Wednesday – Pull Prep
  • 🔁 3 rounds:
  • 10 Superman Holds
  • 10 Reverse Snow Angels
  • 30s Jump Rope
Thursday – Active Recovery
  • Gentle yoga or mobility drills
  • Focus on breath and flexibility
Friday – Full Body
  • 🔁 5 rounds:
  • 30s Jump Rope
  • 10 Squats
  • 10 Push-ups
  • 10 Sit-ups
Saturday – Calisthenics Intro
  • 🔁 3 rounds:
  • 5 Negative Push-ups (lower slowly)
  • 5 Wall Walks
  • 10 Jump Squats
Sunday – Rest
  • Stretch deeply
  • Hydrate and reflect
  • Prepare for Week 3
